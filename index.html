About

I am Iorie Chua, a history enthusiast, philosopher (maybe), scientist, and occasionally—when the stars align—a programmer :). I am currently diving headfirst into the vast, chaotic, and endlessly fascinating world of web development, with a particular obsession for Javascript. As you can probably tell, I thrive on curiosity, constantly exploring fields that ignite my passion. This insatiable drive to learn and create keeps me fueled in my pursuit of eudaimonia (that’s Greek for "flourishing" or "success," in case you were wondering). Whether it’s unraveling the mysteries of the past, pondering the big questions of existence, or tinkering with lines of code, I’m always chasing that spark of inspiration. And hey, if I can build something cool along the way, even better!
